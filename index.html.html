Yes. The pilot is complimentary for all five founding participants. P.A.S.A. absorbs all production, filming, editing, and delivery costs. In exchange, we ask for your honest feedback and a short testimonial after your Spotlight airs. No payment is required at any stage of the pilot.
The Spotlight activates on a race win. If your horse does not win across the 6 pilot meetings, the segment does not air — but your founding member status is retained, and you receive priority access at preferred rates when the programme expands to include "placed" finishes (top 3) from Tier 2 onwards. We strongly recommend applying with a horse that has active entries across multiple pilot meetings.
The pilot runs across 6 consecutive race meetings at Greyville Racecourse and/or Scottsville Racecourse in KwaZulu-Natal. Our crew will be on-site at each meeting. We will confirm your specific race entries with you during the onboarding process in Week 1–2 of the programme.
A 2-minute live segment filmed in the winner's circle immediately after your race win. It extends the existing GallopTV owner interview format into a branded "Winner's Business Spotlight" — with your business name, logo, and website displayed as a lower-third graphic, and a simple fan offer if you choose to include one. The segment airs on GallopTV and Tellytrack during the post-race window. You will approve the graphics and segment format before the first broadcast.
The Punter Justice Fund is P.A.S.A.'s proprietary fairness mechanism. A portion of programme revenue is ring-fenced into the fund, which is deployed as betting wallet credits for affected punters when NHA disciplinary rulings or jockey fines impact race outcomes. Credits remain within the licensed betting ecosystem. Full fund governance details are available on request.
Owner onboarding runs in Weeks 1–2 following confirmation of all five founding participants. Production setup takes place in Week 3. The first live broadcast is targeted for Week 4. We are working toward a 2025 KZN season start date. Apply now to secure your slot and we will confirm your specific onboarding timeline within 48 hours.
